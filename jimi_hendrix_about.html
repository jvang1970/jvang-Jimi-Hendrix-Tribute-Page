Who is Jimi Hendrix?

James Marshal "Jimi" Hendrix (Born legally as Johnny Allen Hendrix) Jimi Hendrix was born on November 27th 1942. was an American musician, singer, and songwriter. Although his mainstream career spanned only four years, he is widely regarded as one of the most influential electric guitarists in the history of popular music, and one of the most celebrated musicians of the 20th century.
